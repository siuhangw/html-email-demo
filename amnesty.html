North Korea Detains South Korean TV Producer for Nearly 50 Years
Hwang Won, a former South Korean television producer, has not been able to return to South Korea for nearly 50 years after his plane was hijacked to North Korea on December 11, 1969. His disappearance is closely linked to North Korea's long-standing abduction of South Korean nationals. Hwang was on his way to Seoul from Gangneung when the plane was hijacked shortly after takeoff, eventually forcing him and other passengers to stay in North Korea. Although 39 passengers were released on February 14, 1970, 11 people, including Hwang Won, were still detained in North Korea...
